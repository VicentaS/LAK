Chapter 3: Luphia capital of Alaron kingdom 2

Lia reached her destination, and hurried inside the jewelry store.
“Welcome lady Kaelia! I hope your way here was safe and smooth.” Store owner greeted Lia.
“Thank you sir.” She nodded and said “It was.”
“Are you here regarding your order you placed a month ago?”
“Yes, is it ready yet?”
“Yes, it was ready for a while now, but as you requested I kept it here. Give me a moment.” store owner asked his assistant to show Lia the brooch she had ordered.
“Here, just as you requested” he showed her the brooch “Light blue color, just like clear sky at day time, with a shape like a moon. Also with gold platting around it. And an engraving on back says [To my dear sister Lexie.] Are you satisfied with the work of our master?”
“Yes, I am. It’s a beautiful brooch. Tell your master he did an amazing job.”
“You’re too kind lady Kaelia.”
As she was about to take her bag of gold out of a pocket on her dress, she realized.
‘Oh no, where is it. I can’t find it.’ Then she remembered a time when she bumped on that starnger on the street.
‘No, it can’t be. Did I drop it there? I have to hurry back. But I don’t have time.’ As she was panicking, the store owner said.
“I see, you don’t have a maid with you. Should I request payment from Stewart family instead? It’s not a big deal. Stewart is a trusted family all around the kingdom.”
‘But I can’t, I wanted it to be a secret. If father will see this bill, he will ask both of us who purchased it, and Lexie will guess what kind of gift I have prepared for her.’ As she panicked even more, a stranger in cloak appeared behind her.
“Lady, you dropped it on the street.” The stranger gave her a bag with gold, with every last gold coin in it.
She couldn’t speak for a moment. ‘How can someone see a bag filled with gold and return it to its owner, without even thinking of keeping it. Maybe he’s really rich, or just a stupidly kind person. Or both.’
“Is everything alright lady Stewart? Do you know this man?” Store owner spoke to Lia, and she returned from her thoughts.
“Ah yes, I’m alright.” Lia looked at the stranger. “Thank you very much kind sir. I will not forget your kindness. I shall repay you for your kind action.”
Stranger smiled.
“No need my lady. I just did what was right. But if you could, please be careful next time. It’s dangerous to walk the streets without any guards. Especially for a noble lady as yourself.”
‘Oh, I can protect myself. You don’t have to worry about me. But...’
“...thank you sir.”
“Then, I shall be going.”
“May I know your name kind sir?” Asked Lia.
The stranger looked a bit surprised. “I’m just a friendly stranger”
‘A friendly what now? Alright, don’t want to give me your name, that’s fine.’
“Take care of yourself as well friendly sir” politely spoke Lia.
As the stranger was about to leave the store, he turned around and looked at Lia who was giving gold coins to the store owner.
‘I swear I saw her somewhere. The store owner said she’s from the Stewart family. Shall I attend the upcoming party as well, though maybe not officially?’
And the stranger left thinking what should he do.
Lia paid for the brooch and hurried back to the carriage.
‘I hope she is not there yet. I have to hurry!’
The carriage was still there, and still no sight of other knights that Lexie took with her.
‘I made it on time. Thanks God!’
She knocked on window on the other side of the carriage really quite so only the maid inside could hear that. Maid opened the window for her, and Lia hopped inside the carriage, without making any noise, so that the guards on the other side wouldn’t hear her.
“Nothing bad happened while I was away I hope?” Lia asked the maid.
“Kaelia, you’re fine! Everything was fine. Guards didn’t notice your absence. Did you get what you went for?”
“Yes, my sisters brooch was ready. Everything went smoothly.”
“But it took you longer than I expected.”
“Yes, I lost my bag of gold on the way there. So I had to go back to find it. Thankfully I found it untouched.”
‘I can’t tell her I went to the smith and met a strangely kind person on my way. She will start questioning me...’
“Oh my, I’m glad you’re fine. I don’t know what I would do if something have happened to you.”
“Heh, no need to worry. You know I can take care of myself.” Lia smiled at the maid.
“Yes, but..”
“Sister, I’m back! Sorry to keep you waiting for so long.”
Lexie entered the carriage in the middle of their conversation.
“It’s about time sister. Did you visit the place you wanted to?”
“Yes, I’m finished in the city for today. But are you sure you don’t want to go anywhere else today?”
Exhausted Kaelia nodded. “I’m sure. I already have everything I needed. Let’s head home if you’re done.”
“Yes, I’m exhausted. And father should be home soon too” Lexie smiled at Lia and ordered the carriage to head back to the mansion.
‘Oh, I’m so exhausted sister. I hope you’ll like the gift I have prepared for you.’ Lia closed her eyes and smiled softly leaning of her hand supported by her knees.
Carriage was moving towards the mansion, they entered the forest that surrounded mansion, passed the village in Stewart family land. And finally arrived at the mansion.
Few hours before...
Duke Kane left the mansion for the royal palace. He arrived there after a while. When he got there a tall man with pitch black hair and deep blue eyes was waiting for Kane.
“You’re here Duke Kane, I hope your way here was comfortable.”
“Thank you advisor Alfred.”
It was kings advisor Alfred. With his sharp sight and tall stature standing there, meeting Kane.
“I’ll show you to a room where you can take a rest before the first training starts.”
“Thank you advisor Alfred. I’m in your care now.”
And Alfred along with royal guards made their way with Kane to a resting place.
